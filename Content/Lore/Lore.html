Origins

How AnarchySMP Was Born
𝖇𝖑𝖚𝖊𝖘𝖙ꬽ𝖓𝖊 originally planned AnarchySMP as a survival server with custom crafting recipes, the first name was "ClubMC" intentionally designed to be difficult. His friend CoolCat30 was brought in as the first tester for a preview session. After the test, 𝖇𝖑𝖚𝖊𝖘𝖙ꬽ𝖓𝖊 reset the world and all inventories, and the server officially launched. Despite the hard recipes, CoolCat30 still managed to kill the Ender Dragon first winning the event.
