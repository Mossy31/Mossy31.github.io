The Spear's History

Lost, Recovered, Lost Again
Before crafting the definitive spear, elonMuschio had a first version that became bugged and could no longer be upgraded. A player killed him at spawn and stole it. elonMuschio tricked the thief into a fake trade — offering the mace in exchange — and recovered the spear. Shortly after, a different player killed him at spawn again. The spear was not picked up by the attacker and despawned permanently. elonMuschio then crafted the definitive version during the Limitless Enchants Event.
