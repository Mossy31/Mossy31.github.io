Biography

Macaroni (Discord) plays under the Minecraft username AdPro_piglet and is one of elonMuschio's closest teammates on the server. Beyond combat and survival, Macaroni is best known as the server's dedicated lore writer — authoring in-game books that document events, history, and stories unfolding on AnarchySMP.

Lore Contributions

In-Game Books
Macaroni has written numerous lore books distributed across the server, preserving the history of AnarchySMP for players past and present. These books cover server events, player stories, and the ongoing narrative of the world.
