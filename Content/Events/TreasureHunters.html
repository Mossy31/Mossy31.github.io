How It Worked

Rules
· Find Pink Pigs hidden within a 5,000 × 5,000 radius of spawn (0, 0)
· Pigs can be anywhere — caves, water, floating, on chests, boats, or buildings
· Pigs cannot move or be killed
· Left-click a pig to receive a reward
· Each pig has a 24-hour cooldown
· Rewards include: VIP, VIP+, Kits, Tags, Cosmetics, Items, Illegal Items, Enchants
