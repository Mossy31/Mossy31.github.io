elonMuschio's Mace Crafted during the Limitless Enchants Event · Owner: elonMuschio
A mace enchanted during the Limitless Enchants Event. Equipped with Breach X and Density X, this weapon excels at bypassing armor. The Mending enchantment allows it to self-repair using experience points, making it effectively indestructible in normal use.
