Bedrock (Fylyberto's Gift) Gifted by admin Fylyberto · Owner: elonMuschio
A piece of Bedrock obtainable only through admin commands, gifted to elonMuschio by server admin Fylyberto. This item cannot be placed, equipped, or dropped, and exists solely in elonMuschio's inventory as an untransferable collector's item. The Curse of Binding X prevents it from being equipped or removed from the inventory slot, while the Curse of Vanishing X would destroy it on death — making it a paradoxically inert yet permanent possession.
