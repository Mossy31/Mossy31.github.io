Grapple Hooks (5 Tiers) From the Grappling Hooks Event · ~Late 2025 · Owner: elonMuschio
Custom craftable items introduced during the Grappling Hooks Event. Each hook launches the player on right-click, functioning as a mobility tool for players without fly or hacks. After the event ended, all crafting recipes were permanently disabled, making existing hooks uncraftable rarities. elonMuschio owns one of each tier plus an additional Netherite-tier hook.

Crafting & Tiers
Each tier was crafted using a fishing rod in the center slot of the crafting table, surrounded by ore blocks of the corresponding tier. Higher ore tier yields better performance across all stats.
