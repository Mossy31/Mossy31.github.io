Glowstick Obtained from Treasure Pig · Owner: elonMuschio
A plain stick with a green custom display name and a Knockback X enchantment. It was dropped by a Treasure Pig during the Treasure Hunters Event. No other player is known to own one, making it a one-of-a-kind item on the server. Its extreme Knockback enchantment makes it a surprisingly effective tool for launching other players.
