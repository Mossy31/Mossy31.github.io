Treasure Hunter Tag From the Treasure Hunters Event · Owner: elonMuschio
A cosmetic name tag displayed in-game next to the player's name. The tag was awarded for participation in the Treasure Hunters Event, during which players tracked down and found Treasure Pigs scattered across the world. It has no gameplay effect but serves as a permanent mark of participation in the event.
