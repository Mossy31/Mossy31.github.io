elonMuschio's Spear Crafted during the Limitless Enchants Event · Owner: elonMuschio
A sword enchanted far beyond normal limits during the Limitless Enchants Event. The combination of mutually exclusive enchants (Smite, Bane of Arthropods, and Sharpness all at once) makes this item impossible to replicate through normal gameplay. It is one of the most powerful weapons on the server.
