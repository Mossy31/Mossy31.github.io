Description

Gumball Machines Introduced during the Gumball Machine Event
Custom decorative blocks introduced during the Gumball Machine Event, available in multiple colors. These are purely cosmetic items with no functional gameplay effect. They can be placed as decoration in builds. The event was distributed on Mar 17, 2026, with the winner being idkwhat_to_put through a giveaway.

Bugged Variants

Player Head Variants
15 bugged variants of the Gumball Machine exist that function only as player heads rather than the intended decorative block. These are considered collector's items due to their unintended nature.
