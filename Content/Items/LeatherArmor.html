elonMuschio's Leather Armor ■ #6FA11A Crafted during the Limitless Enchants Event · Owner: elonMuschio
A full set of leather armor dyed in a distinctive olive-green color (#6FA11A) and enchanted during the Limitless Enchants Event. Despite being leather, the Protection X enchantment provides substantial damage reduction. The Mending enchantment keeps the set perpetually repaired. The unusual choice of leather over Netherite makes this armor a notable cosmetic statement on top of its defensive capabilities.
